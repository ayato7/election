Timeline of Chief Samson Gordons Okomitie.

1

In October, 2016, He Hosted a football competition, that comprises of all Secondary Schools in Ughelli South L.G.A. With Ewu Secondary School winning the trophy.

2

In June, 2017 A skill empowerment program for women was carried out across Ughelli South L.G.A with the sum of #100,000 given to each participants. Total of 100 women were beneficiaries.

3

In June 2018, he Commissioned various water project in some communities in Ughelli South, namely Ewu town, Okpare town, Otokutu, Ekakpamre e.t.c
